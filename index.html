What I Value

Accountability

1.Accountability means taking full responsibility for my actions, decisions, and their outcomes.
2.This value derives from my upbringing and school, where I was taught that actions have consequences and that growth comes from owning both successes and failures.
3.Accountability builds trust and credibility. In both academic, and professional, people rely on others to follow through on commitments and act responsibly.
4.Without accountability, trust is lost, making it hard for people to want to depend on you.

Honesty

1. Honesty is the practice of being truthful, transparent, and sincere in both action and speach.
2. Honesty was instilled in me through family values and reinforced through education, where we strongly believed in being truthful at all times even if it may hurt ones freelings.
3. Honesty forms the foundation of trust in relationships and professional settings.
4. Dishonesty can damage reputations, destroy relationships, and even mental health.

Professionalism

1. Professionalism involves acting with respect, responsibility, and ethical awareness in academic and work environments.
2. This value developed through school expectations, boyscouts, and my exposure to professional standards in academic and workplace settings.
3. Professionalism creates a positive environment where people feel respected and confident in each otherâ€™s abilities.
4. A lack of professionalism can lead to damaged reputations, strained relationships, and limited career opportunities.

Service

1. Service means using my skills, knowledge, and time to help others and contribute positively to my community and society.
2. This value originates from family experiences from when I was a child and we would donate our time at church to help feed those who couldn't
3. Service gives purpose to my actions and encourages ethical use of technology. It reminds me that decisions should consider how they affect others, not just personal outcomes.
4. Without a sense of service, actions can become self-centered and harmful.
