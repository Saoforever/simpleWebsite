Why Cybersecurity Matters in Healthcare

The healthcare industry depends on information technology to store and manage highly sensitive patient data. Because this information includes medical histories, personal identifiers, and insurance details, healthcare organizations are frequent targets of cyberattacks.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

One essential best practice is strong access control. Healthcare organizations should use multi-factor authentication and limit access to systems based on job roles. This reduces the risk of unauthorized access and insider threats.

Data encryption and regular backups are also critical. Encrypting patient records protects information even if systems are breached, while secure backups ensure continuity of care during ransomware attacks or system failures.
